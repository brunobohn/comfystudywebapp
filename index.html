An online Pomodoro Timer to boost your productivity.

The ComfyStudy WebApp is a timer that works on desktop & mobile browser. The aim of this app is to help you focus on any task you are working on, such as study, writing, or coding.



This app is inspired by Pomodoro Technique. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.
